This website incorporates several aspects of universal design, including an easy to use navigation bar that displays the webpage you are currently on via the blue box, and is accessible from every page.

This website also features and easily readable sign in and sign up page for logging into your account and creating one respectively. Both pages also have a link to one another seperate from the navbar.

This website has a pleasing design to it aswell, it contains bright colours contrasting the darker ones to make them stand out. A dark background and bright colored text boxes also help one focus on the text.
